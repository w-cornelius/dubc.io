I Am a Giver of Soles

I am hopeful and determined.

I wonder how many more kids I can help.

I see kids with shoes that are falling apart or have blisters on their feet.

I want them to have new shoes that fit, so they can run and play.

I am a Giver of Soles.

I feel sad when I see kids who can't play because of their shoes.

I worry that some kids have to share one pair with their brother or sister.

I cry for the kids at the shelter who don't have any shoes at all.

I am a Giver of Soles.

I say that you are never too young to make a difference.

I dream of giving kids confidence and hope.

I try to send a handwritten card with every new pair of sneakers.

I hope to keep helping everyone, one "sole" at a time.

I am a Giver of Soles.
