The Glowing Tree

(6th Grade Version)

Our hero didn't talk about the sun,
Or stars up in the sky, not one.
He said there is a different kind of light,
That glows inside us, even in the night.

He showed us a big tree that glows so bright,
Its branches are a super awesome light.
It connects us all, so no one is alone,
It’s like a secret that we’ve always known.

The roots go deep and hide far in the ground,
That’s where the power for the light is found.
So when it's dark, remember what he said:
We are the tree, and its light isn't dead.
